Temperature Conversion: Fahrenheit to Kelvin & Celsius

Fahrenheit to Kelvin

Formula: °F=(K-273.15)×9/5+32

To convert Fahrenheit to Kelvin:

  1. Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature.
  2. Multiply the result by 5/9.
  3. Add 273.15 to the result.

Example: Fahrenheit temperature 80°F

Calculation: (80 - 32) * 5/9 + 273.15 = 299.82K

Fahrenheit to Celsius

Formula: °F=(°C×9/5)+32

To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius:

  1. Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature.
  2. Multiply the result by 5/9.

Example: Fahrenheit temperature 80°F

Calculation: (80 - 32) * 5/9 = 26.67°C
