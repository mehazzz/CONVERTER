METHODS FOR CONVERSIONS

Decimal to Binary Conversion

To convert a decimal number to its binary equivalent:

  1. Divide the decimal number by 2.
  2. Write down the remainder (0 or 1).
  3. Continue dividing the quotient by 2 until the quotient becomes 0.
  4. Write the remainders in reverse order to get the binary number.

Example: Decimal number 10

Calculation: 10 / 2 = 5 remainder 0
5 / 2 = 2 remainder 1
2 / 2 = 1 remainder 0
1 / 2 = 0 remainder 1

Binary number: 1010

Octal to Binary Conversion

To convert an octal number to its binary equivalent:

  1. Convert each octal digit to its 3-bit binary representation.
  2. Combine the binary representations to get the final binary number.

Example: Octal number 72

Conversion: Octal 7 (111) Octal 2 (010)

Binary number: 111010

Hexadecimal to Binary Conversion

To convert a hexadecimal number to its binary equivalent:

  1. Convert each hexadecimal digit to its 4-bit binary representation.
  2. Combine the binary representations to get the final binary number.

Example: Hexadecimal number 1A3

Conversion: Hexadecimal 1 (0001) Hexadecimal A (1010) Hexadecimal 3 (0011)

Binary number: 000110100011
