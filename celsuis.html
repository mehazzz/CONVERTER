Temperature Conversion: Celsius to Kelvin & Fahrenheit

Celsius to Kelvin

To convert celsuis to kelvin

Formula: °C=K-273.15

  1. Simply add 273.15 to the Celsius temperature.

Example: Celsius temperature 25°C

Calculation: 25 + 273.15 = 298.15K

Celsius to Fahrenheit

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit:

Formula: °C=(°F-32)×5/9

  1. Multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5.
  2. Add 32 to the result.

Example: Celsius temperature 25°C

Calculation: (25 * 9/5) + 32 = 77°F
