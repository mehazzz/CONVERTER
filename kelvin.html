Temperature Conversion: Kelvin to Celsius & Fahrenheit

Kelvin to Celsius

Formula: K=°C+273.15

To convert Kelvin to Celsius:

  1. Simply subtract 273.15 from the Kelvin temperature.

Example: Kelvin temperature 300K

Calculation: 300 - 273.15 = 26.85°C

Kelvin to Fahrenheit

Formula: K=(°F-32)×5/9+273.15

To convert Kelvin to Fahrenheit:

  1. Subtract 273.15 from the Kelvin temperature.
  2. Multiply the result by 9/5.
  3. Add 32 to the result.

Example: Kelvin temperature 300K

Calculation: (300 - 273.15) * 9/5 + 32 = 80.33°F
